Tanmay Ghai

I am a M.S. Candidate in the computer science department at the Viterbi School of Engineering at the University of Southern California. I did my bachelor's at the University of California, Berkeley where I graduated from in May 2019.

I am also currently a Software Development Engineer at Workday, a SaaS ERP solution for the cloud. Previously, I interned at Workday as well as ServiceNow, another enterprise cloud computing platform.

My research interests are in the intersection of artificial intelligence and it's applications to real-world problems. More specifically, I love reinforcement learning, statistical machine learning, and bioinformatics. Previously, I've been a part of the Integrated Circuits & Systems Group, advised by Prof. Ajay Joshi and Wei Wang's Lab at UCSD, advised by Dr. Wei Wang and Dr. Rizi Ai.
